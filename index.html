Manifesto

Our ideal business is:

  1. SaaS (70%+ recurring revenue)
    You've got a product that people love and pay for every month.

  2. Small ($100k - $1m annual revenue)
    You've got a few customers, but you're not yet a household name.

  3. Profitable ($50k - $500k annual profit)
    You're making 50%+ profit margins with minimal overhead.

  4. Growing (5%+ MoM revenue growth)
    You're growing quickly, yet sustainably.

  5. Simple (<5 full-time employees)
    You're either a solo founder or have a small team.

Sound like your company? Let's talk. Reach out via the Contact page.
